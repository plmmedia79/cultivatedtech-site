03 · Our Approach

Four pillars that shape every engagement.

A small firm with strong opinions. These aren't slogans — they're the things we will not compromise on, and the reason clients come back.

01

Discovery-First Engineering

No code before we understand the problem. Every engagement begins with a one-to-two-week discovery phase: stakeholder interviews, system mapping, risk assessment, and a written brief you can take to your board.

02

Senior Engineers Only

Every line of code is written by an engineer with significant production experience. We don't staff junior developers on client engagements and we don't subcontract delivery offshore.

03

Transparent Communication

Weekly written status reports. A shared backlog you can read at any time. Demos every Friday. If something is slipping, you'll hear it from us before you'd notice it yourself.

04

Long-Term Partnership

We design code, contracts, and documentation so that another team — yours or someone else's — can pick up where we left off. We're optimizing for your tenth year, not our next invoice.

05 · Why Cultivated Technology Group

A different posture toward client work.

We optimize for outcomes you can measure and code you can keep. The list below is what we mean by that, in concrete terms.

i.

Quality over volume

We take on a deliberately small number of engagements. The work the firm accepts is the work the firm can finish well.

ii.

Written everything

Decisions, trade-offs, and assumptions live in writing. New hires on your side can read the project history and understand it.

iii.

Production discipline

CI, tests, observability, runbooks, and a deployment story are baseline, not extras. We treat the day after launch as part of the project.

iv.

Honest scoping

We're transparent about what we don't know yet. Fixed bids on uncertain work are how good firms ruin good projects.

v.

Your code, your IP

Full ownership transfers to you on payment, including infrastructure access, source control, and documentation.

vi.

US-based engineering

The firm operates from California. Delivery happens on US business hours, with US-based principals accountable for the work.
