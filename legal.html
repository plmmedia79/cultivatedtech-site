How to Verify This Information

The corporate identification information above is filed with and maintained by the California Secretary of State. Any member of the public may verify the entity's existence, status, and filings at no cost using the California Business Search portal. To do so:

  1. Visit https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/search/business.
  2. Select "Entity Number" as the search criteria and enter 4701299; or select "Keyword" and enter Cultivated Technology Group.
  3. Open the resulting record to view the entity's status, registration date, registered office address, and agent for service of process.

Regulatory & Reviewer Notes

For Know-Your-Business (KYB), Anti-Money-Laundering (AML), Customer Due Diligence (CDD), and Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) reviewers, the following operational facts may be useful in completing a review:

  • The firm is a US-domiciled professional-services corporation. Its principal activity is the design, development, and delivery of custom software systems on a fee-for-service basis.
  • The firm's clientele consists predominantly of established US businesses. New business clients are subject to the firm's own onboarding process, summarized on the Our Process page.
  • The firm does not provide regulated financial services, payment processing, custodial services, securities advice, legal advice, medical advice, or any other regulated professional service outside of software engineering.
  • The firm does not issue, transact in, or custody any form of cryptocurrency, digital asset, or other virtual currency for itself or on behalf of clients.
  • The firm's Terms of Service are governed by the laws of the State of California, United States. Disputes are resolved by binding arbitration seated in California.
  • The firm's Privacy Policy describes its processing of personal information and is intended to be compliant with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent regional regimes to the extent they apply.
